FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


How long is an escape session? - You have 60 minutes to escape the vault!
How will I know what I can and cannot touch? - Forbidden items to touch will be marked with a pink diamond.
How many people are allowed in a vault and will I be with any other groups? - 8 people are allowed in each room and if there is room left and other people book that vault you will work with them.
Are electronic devices permitted in the vault? - Sorry, no electronic devices are permitted in the vault, this includes cell phones and cameras.
Do we get any hints? If so, how many? - Yes, you get three hints.
